Delivery Information

We use a specialist carrier called Fragilistics to fulfill our deliveries. They service the UK Mainland every week by fulfilling to certain areas on set days every week. Below you will find a map broken down into the postcode areas within the UK, the delivery area that they fall into and, the dedicated delivery schedule for that area.

We endeavour to deliver all goods within 6 working days although some products may take longer. Orders received on Saturdays, Sundays or public holidays will be processed the next working day Fragilistics deliver to every UK postcode at least once a week.

When placing your order, please ensure that you have included the full delivery address details including an accurate postcode, mobile phone number and an up to date e-mail address. You will be notified of delivery times via text and e-mail. IF THE DELIVERY FAILS BECAUSE OF FAILURE TO PROVIDE THESE THEN A 350 Rupees REDELIVERY CHARGE WILL BE REQUIRED. If then the order is cancelled a 350 Rupees failed delivery charge will be taken from the refund.

Once your order is placed we will endeavour to deliver this to you as soon and as safely as possible. Please note we cannot accept responsibility for delivery delays due to adverse weather conditions or abnormal traffic restrictions. Mirror Outlet are open 7 days a week and Fragilistics deliver all year round with the exception of the holiday period between Christmas and New Year. Deliveries will cease on the day prior to Christmas eve and resume on the 1st working day after New year. If you would like further delivery details please contact us on 0340 8315149 If you have any special requirements or believe access to your address may be difficult for our delivery driver then please contact us on dispatch@fragilistics.co.pk

Our deliveries are carried out by our specialised mirror glass courier called Fragilistics. It is a one man delivery service. For large items / mirror-glass, the driver will require assistance in getting the item from the van to the property. Please note the drivers are only permitted to carry out ground floor deliveries. For deliveries to apartments where communal stairs need to be negotiated in order to access the property, the drivers will assist where possible subject to unencumbered access. (Our Delivery company or Driver are not liable for any damage caused to the mirror or property while assisting further than the front door). Fragilistics are a pro-active company and will always endevour to assist where possible. Where access is restricted the delivery drivers reserve the right to refuse assistance on safety grounds. In the event of a customer being unable/unwilling to assist, the item will be returned to us and you will be contacted in due course by a member of our dispatch team.
